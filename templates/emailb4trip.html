Passports

Please make sure you (and anyone else in your group) has sent us a scan of their passport. We need it in order to process harbour clearence so that by the time you arrive the boat is ready to go without delay. Please remember to bring the passport with you.

Travel Insurance

Please make sure you (and anyone else in your group) has sent us a scan of their travel insurance (see our terms for further info as to the kind of travel insurance required). If you haven't already done so, please inform us of any medical issues you may have.

Beer

We provide {BEER_FREE_CRATES} crates of beer free. If you are a thirsty bunch then please let us know in advance and we will carry extra on the boat (cost per additional crate is {BEER_CRATE_COST}).

Dietary requirements

Now is a good time to let us know if you or any of your group have any particular dietary requirements (e.g. someone is diabetic, someone is vegan). Indonesia does not have many of the foods you may be familiar with so it can sometimes be difficult for us to source stuff at last minute. Informing us well in advance is very helpful.

Surf Guiding

A surf guide will accompany the boat. Their role is to ensure that you get the best waves possible, informing you the night before what the plan is for the next day, explaining a new surf spot and accompanying you in the water to be at hand if needed. Feel free to ask them questions at any time. While they are not there as surf-coaches they will be happy to offer advice and tips so don't be shy about asking!

Fishing

We carry fishing equipment on board: rods, reels, lures, spearguns... However if you are a keen fisherman we advise you to bring your own gear. We will endeavour to have a fishing guide on the trip with you to offer advice on where and when is good to fish, the kinds of fish around and someone to accompany you trawling or spearing if desired.
